Portfolio/Projects

Business Manager

Business Manager is a client-server application designed to simplify and improve the management of personnel, products, orders, and clients within a company. This project was developed as part of a collaborative effort, where I contributed to both the front-end and back-end development. The application features a user-friendly graphical interface connected to a robust database, allowing users to perform various operations such as adding, modifying, deleting, and viewing records. It also includes advanced functionalities like automated data pre-filling, date pickers, and role-based access control, ensuring that only authorized personnel can perform specific actions.

The application is ideal for businesses looking to manage their internal operations efficiently. It can be used by HR managers to handle employee data, inventory managers to track products, and sales teams to manage client orders. The system also includes a statistics module, enabling users to generate reports and analyze key metrics such as monthly revenue and client purchase history.

Craft Creator

Craft Creator is a Minecraft mod designed to make the process of creating custom recipes for modpacks more efficient and user-friendly. With this mod, content creators no longer need to write complex code manually. Instead, Craft Creator automates the entire process, generating DataPacks with just a few clicks. The mod supports recipes for Thermal machines, standard Minecraft crafting, and Botania, with plans to expand support to Mekanism and Create in the future.

The mod features an intuitive GUI where users can simply place items into slots, configure settings, and generate recipes instantly. This eliminates the need for typing out lengthy code, making it accessible for both beginners and experienced modpack creators. Additionally, Craft Creator includes a recipe deletion feature, allowing users to easily remove unwanted recipes with a simple interface.

Craft Creator is specifically designed to assist modpack creators by reducing the time and effort required to write and manage custom recipes. It’s perfect for anyone looking to enhance their Minecraft experience without the hassle of coding. Whether you’re creating a small custom pack or a large modpack, Craft Creator ensures a smooth and efficient recipe-making journey.

Technologies :

PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, Apache Server

Worldwide Weather Watcher

This project focuses on developing a weather station that can collect and record meteorological data. The system is designed to operate in different modes, including standard, configuration, maintenance, and energy-saving, allowing users to customize its functionality based on their needs. Data is acquired at regular intervals and stored on an SD card for later analysis.

The weather station includes various sensors to measure parameters like temperature, humidity, pressure, and light intensity. It also features an LED color-coded system for easy status monitoring and error detection. Users can modify system parameters through the configuration mode, ensuring flexibility in data acquisition. Overall, the project demonstrates practical applications of embedded systems and sensor integration, offering a hands-on learning experience in electronics and programming.

Technologies :

C Arduino, Linux, Bash

Stage Finder

StageFinder is designed to make the process of finding internships for students easier and more efficient. This platform offers a comprehensive and user-friendly interface where users with appropriate permissions can create, delete, and modify internship offers, ensuring that the most up-to-date opportunities are always available. Additionally, the system allows for the management of enterprises, teachers, and student profiles, enabling administrators to add, remove, or modify details as needed.

To further enhance the user experience, StageFinder includes a wishlist feature, allowing students to save and track their favorite internship opportunities for easy access and future reference. This functionality empowers students to organize their preferences and stay focused on the opportunities that align best with their career goals. StageFinder not only simplifies the internship search process but also provides a centralized hub for managing all related entities, making it an invaluable tool for students, educators, and businesses alike.

Technologies :

PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, Apache Server
